Our Vision

Continually monitors business processes and their application behaviours as well as requirements to always ensure the availability of the required infrastructure needs e.g. compute and network

Through advanced analytics, learns and predicts when capability is required or can be removed thus always optimizing the infrastructure requirements for the business function Self-provisions and decommissions capacity to match business functional needs.

Proactively monitors and ‘self-heals’ through automated workload management – notifying of malfunctioning components that are taken out of services for remediation.

Continually analyses and optimizes the underlying infrastructure services to ensure cost efficiencies and operational effectiveness—i.e. : swapping cloud service providers to always drive the most cost optimized and efficient service provider.

Can promote applications, indeed whole business processes, between pre-production and production to seamlessly introduce new services with negligible disruption.
